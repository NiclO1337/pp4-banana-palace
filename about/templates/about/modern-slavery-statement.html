Modern Slavery Statement

Banana Palace restaurant is committed to combating modern slavery and human trafficking in all its forms. We recognize that slavery, forced labor, and human trafficking are abhorrent practices that have no place in our business operations or supply chains.

Our Commitment

Zero Tolerance: Banana Palace restaurant maintains a zero-tolerance policy towards modern slavery and human trafficking. We are committed to ensuring that our business and supply chain are free from any form of exploitation or coercion.

Awareness and Training: We provide training and awareness programs to our employees to educate them about the signs of modern slavery and their responsibilities in preventing and reporting any suspected cases.

Supplier Due Diligence: We conduct due diligence assessments of our suppliers and business partners to ensure that they share our commitment to ethical and responsible business practices. We expect our suppliers to comply with all applicable laws and regulations regarding labor rights and human rights.

Monitoring and Compliance: We regularly monitor and review our supply chain to identify and address any potential risks of modern slavery or human trafficking. We work closely with our suppliers to implement corrective actions and improve labor standards where necessary.

Reporting and Whistleblowing

Reporting Mechanisms: Banana Palace restaurant encourages all employees, suppliers, and stakeholders to report any concerns or suspicions of modern slavery or human trafficking. We have established confidential reporting mechanisms to facilitate the reporting of such incidents.

Non-Retaliation: We prohibit retaliation against any individual who reports in good faith concerns about modern slavery or human trafficking. We are committed to investigating all reports promptly and taking appropriate action to address any identified issues.

Continuous Improvement

Collaboration: Banana Palace restaurant collaborates with industry peers, government agencies, and non-governmental organizations to share best practices and enhance our efforts to combat modern slavery and human trafficking.

Continuous Monitoring: We are committed to continuous improvement in our policies, procedures, and practices related to modern slavery prevention. We regularly review and update our policies to reflect emerging trends and regulatory requirements.

Our Responsibility

Banana Palace restaurant recognizes that we have a responsibility to uphold human rights and ethical labor practices in all aspects of our business operations. We are committed to working tirelessly to eradicate modern slavery and human trafficking and create a world where all individuals are treated with dignity, respect, and equality.

Contact Us
