Careers at Banana Palace

Join the Banana Palace team and embark on an exciting journey in the hospitality industry! We are always on the lookout for talented and passionate individuals who share our commitment to delivering exceptional dining experiences and promoting a culture of excellence.

Our Culture

At Banana Palace, we believe in fostering a supportive and inclusive work environment where every team member is valued, respected, and empowered to succeed. Our culture is built on the following core principles:

  • Teamwork: We believe in the power of collaboration and teamwork to achieve our goals and deliver outstanding service to our guests.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in everything we do, from the quality of our food and service to the professionalism of our staff.
  • Diversity and Inclusion: We celebrate diversity and embrace differences in perspectives, backgrounds, and experiences. We believe that a diverse team leads to innovation and creativity.
  • Continuous Learning: We are committed to providing opportunities for professional growth and development to our employees through training, mentorship, and ongoing learning opportunities.

Our Policies

Equal Employment Opportunity: Banana Palace is an equal opportunity employer and prohibits discrimination and harassment of any kind. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workplace where all employees are treated with fairness, dignity, and respect.

Workplace Health and Safety: We prioritize the health, safety, and well-being of our employees and guests. We adhere to strict health and safety protocols to ensure a safe working environment for everyone.

Ethical Conduct: We conduct our business with integrity, honesty, and transparency. We expect all employees to adhere to the highest ethical standards and comply with all applicable laws and regulations.

Join Our Team

If you are passionate about hospitality and are interested in joining the Banana Palace team, we invite you to explore our current job openings and submit your application. To apply for a job, please send your CV and cover letter to careers@bananapalace.com. Be sure to include the position you are applying for in the subject line of your email. If the position you are applying for is not listed in current job openings then write "Spontaneous Application" in the subject line and we will contact you as soon as possible to discuss your future with us.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests for Banana Palace restaurant, please contact us at info@bananapalace.com or +22 (6)262 725 232.
